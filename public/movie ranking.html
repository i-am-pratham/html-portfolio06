The Best Movies According to Prathamesh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  • Your Name(2016)

    • This is My first ever anime movie. The story revolve around the two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

  • Harry Potter(2001-2011)

    • There are total 8 Harry Potter movies.All are the best. The movies are about lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. This movies are completely about magic so if you love magical world you should give it a try.

  • Jab We Met(2007)

    • It is my one of my favourite Bollywood movie.It is aboutAditya, a heartbroken business tycoon, aimlessly boards a train to escape his depressing life. He meets Geet, a bubbly Punjabi girl, and gets pulled into her crazy life.
